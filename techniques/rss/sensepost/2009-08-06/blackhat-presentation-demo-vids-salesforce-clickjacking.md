 Goal 
 The premise behind this video was that while we are migrating more and more services into the cloud, the front-end through which the services are accessed as well as managed is (in many cases) a web application and we still have not figured out how to write secure web applications reliably. The implication is that business-critical services and infrastructure maybe at risk due to a web developer&#8217;s mistake.
