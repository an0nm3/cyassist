When doing internals, usually an easy first step is to use Responder and wait to retrieve NTLM hashes, cracking them and hoping for a weak password. 
 The problem is that sometimes fancy cracking rigs might not be available, it might be a mess to copy/paste all those hashes, send them, wait for an answer where you could already do some work locally, without any effort. We&#8217;re all lazy, and I&#8217;m even more lazy. That&#8217;s why I decided doing this project.
