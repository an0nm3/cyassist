Like it, hate it or just plain struggling to understand it, Twitter has made a huge impact across a wide range of fields. We use it fairly heavily internally for simulated water-cooler chatter and quick link-exchange. (like any piece of sp-geek-over-engineering we also have a tweet-bot to convert tweets to emails, and convert blog notifications to tweets). It&#8217;s pretty clear though, that once we started tweeting internally, people started blogging less. There&#8217;s something liberating about saying &#8220;here&#8217;s a link&#8221;, as opposed to taking the time to formulate your thoughts into a full blown posting.
