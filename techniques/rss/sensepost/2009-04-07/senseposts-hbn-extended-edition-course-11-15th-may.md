We have scheduled our next training course, Hacking By Numbers – Extended Edition  (Bootcamp)  in   May 11-15th . 
 The course runs for a full 5 days. 
 Overview 
 The HBN &#8216;Extended Edition&#8217; is simply an intensive extended version of the regular Bootcamp course. Whilst the content and structure are essentially the same as Bootcamp, the Extended Edition offer students a deeper understanding of the concepts being presented and affords them more time to practice the techniques being taught. Extended Edition is currently offered in Switzerland and South Africa only, or can be arranged on request.
