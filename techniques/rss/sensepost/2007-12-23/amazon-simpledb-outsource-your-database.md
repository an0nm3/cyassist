Amazon announced the  beta of Amazon SimpleDB  without that much fanfare, but it is an interesting trend to watch.. 
 Essentially amazon are giving the power of a database to people used to excel and simple queries, backed by their massively optimised infrastructure. It will make popping up a web shop even more trivial than it has been in the past, and i guess continues along the growing trend of allowing &#8220;content to be king&#8221;. i.e. u dont need a sql geek in your corner, just a good idea .
