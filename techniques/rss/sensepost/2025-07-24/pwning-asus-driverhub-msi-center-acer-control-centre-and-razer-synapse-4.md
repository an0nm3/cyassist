At the beginning of this year I spent a week finding several vulnerabilities in various &#8220;bloatware&#8221; software. This was after I got suspicious of how my ASUS motherboard&#8217;s &#8220;DriverHub&#8221; behaved. In the end I looked at 6 targets from 6 random vendors (apart from ASUS) and found vulnerabilities ranging from Remote Code Execution to Local Privilege Escalation in all of them. Those were: ASUS, Acer, Lenovo, HP, MSI and Razer.
