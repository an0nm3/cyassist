With the recent buzz around the  iMessage crypto bug  from the John&#8217;s Hopkins team, several people pointed out that you would  need a root CA  to make it work. While getting access to the private key for a global root CA is probably hard, getting a device to trust a malicious root CA is sometimes phrased as difficult to do, but really isn&#8217;t. (There&#8217;s a brief technical note about this in the caveats section at the end.)
