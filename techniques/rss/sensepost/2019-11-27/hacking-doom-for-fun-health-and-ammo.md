Remember  iddqd  and  idkfa ? Those are two strings were etched into my brain at a very young age where fond memories of playing shareware Doom live. For SenseCon &#8217;19, Lauren and Reino joined me as we dove into some reversing of  chocolate-doom  with the aim of recreating similar cheats. The results? Well, a video of it is shown below. We managed to get cheats working that would: 
  Increment your ammo instead of decrement it.  Increment everyone&#8217;s health for the amount it would have gone down for. Yes, you read right, everyone.  Toggle cheats just like how they behaved in classic doom.  
 The source code for our cheats live  here  if you want to play along, or maybe even contribute new ones :)
