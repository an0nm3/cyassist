Sometimes you need to get in the way of a hardware device and its controller, and see what it has to say for itself. If you are lucky, the two parts are communicating using a serial port, and then it&#8217;s relatively simple to do. In this post, I will explain two scenarios where I had to do this, and the approach that I took in each. As a bonus, I&#8217;ll also show some hardware that I put together to make it easier.
