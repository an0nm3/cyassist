So&#8230; Black Hat DC is rushing at us like a speeding big&#8230; speeding thing. This is just a friendly a reminder about the show ( Hyatt Regency Crystal City • February 16-19 ). We have two courses on offer at the DC show this year &#8211;  Bootcamp  (a highly practical course that teaches method-based hacker thinking, skills and techniques) and  Combat  (all hack, no talk &#8211; our flagship course). 
 One small change to our usual approach this time is that we&#8217;re requesting Combat students to bring their own laptops. On Bootcamp and our other courses we provide pre-configured XP boxes but Combat participants are generally already quite experienced and comfortable on their own platforms.
