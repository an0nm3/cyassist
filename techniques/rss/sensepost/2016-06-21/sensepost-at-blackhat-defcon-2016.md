The annual  Hacker Summer Camp  is nearly upon us, everyone at SensePost is getting ready. This is a brief overview of what we&#8217;ll be doing. The  tl;dr  is: BlackHat Training, BlackHat Arsenal x2, Defcon talk &amp; Stickers :) 
 BlackHat Training 
 We&#8217;re back at BlackHat for our 15th year of training with a selection of courses ranging from introductory courses for beginners through to hardcore courses for experts. 
 
  Basic Tools &amp; Techniques for Hackers &#8211; Beginner Level  
  Mobile Application Bootcamp &#8211; Journeyman Level  
  Web Application Bootcamp &#8211; Journeyman Level  
  Black Ops Hacking for Pentesters &#8211; Master Level  
  Threat Intelligence using Maltego  This one isn&#8217;t ours, but our good friends and business partners, Paterva :) 
 
 BlackHat Arsenal 
 We were fortunate enough to have two tools accepted for  BlackHat Arsenal  this year. We think building open source tools for the hacker community is an important part of how we roll, and we appreciate  ToolsWatch  and the  NETpeas  crews efforts with arsenal.
