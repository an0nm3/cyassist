For the last year, Glenn and I have been obsessed with our phones; especially with regard to the data being leaked by a  device that is always with you, powered on and often provided with a fast Internet connection. From this obsession, the  Snoopy framework  was born and released. 
 After  44con  this year,  Channel 4   contacted us to be part of a new experimental show named &#8216; Data Baby &#8216;, whose main goal is to grab ideas from the security community, and transform them into an easy-to-understand concept screened to the public during the 7 o&#8217;clock news.
