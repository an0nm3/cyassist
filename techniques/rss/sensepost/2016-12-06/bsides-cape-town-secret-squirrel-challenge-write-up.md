Last weekend was the BSides Cape Town conference, currently ZA&#8217;s only hacker con. It&#8217;s a cool little con with big dreams that get a little closer each time. This year was a lot a fun and well put together, congrats to all of the speakers organisers and volunteers. 
 SP gave some talks; Charl spoke about where we&#8217;re headed in a talk entitled  Love Triangles in CyberSpace; a tale about trust in 5 chapters . Chris discussed his  DLL preloading work  and released  his toolset . Finally, Darryn &amp; Thomas spoke about  exploiting unauth&#8217;ed X sessions  and released  their tool XRDP , it was also their first con talk ever.
