Over at [ Rational Survivability ] beaker as coined the term  EDoS . To describe how &#8220;the utility and agility of the cloud computing models such as Amazon AWS (EC2/S3) and the pricing models that go along with them can actually pose a very nasty risk to those who use the cloud to provide service&#8221; 
 Of course, this has kicked off the flurry of responses from &#8220;How is this different to soaking up the bandwidth of people who pay per gig&#8221; to &#8220;OMG! thats the new thing.. Cloud Computing is bad&#8221;.
