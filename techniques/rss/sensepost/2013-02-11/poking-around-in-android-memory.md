Taking inspiration from Vlad&#8217;s  post  I&#8217;ve been playing around with alternate means of viewing traffic/data generated by Android apps. 
 The technique that has given me most joy is memory analysis. Each application on android is run in the Dalvik VM and is allocated it&#8217;s own heap space. Android being android, free and open, numerous ways of dumping the contents of the application heap exist.
