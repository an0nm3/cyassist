This year, for the fourth time, myself and some others here at SensePost have worked together with the team from  ITWeb  in the planning of their annual  Security Summit . A commercial conference is always (I suspect) a delicate balance between the different drivers from business, technology and &#8216;industry&#8217;, but this year&#8217;s event is definitely our best effort thus far. ITWeb has more than ever acknowledged the centrality of good, objective content and has worked closely with us as the  Technical Committee  and their various  sponsors  to strike the optimal balance. I don&#8217;t think we have it 100% right yet, and there are some improvements and initiatives that will unfortunately only manifest at next year&#8217;s event, but this year&#8217;s program ( here  and  here ) is nevertheless first class and comparable with almost anything else I&#8217;ve seen.
