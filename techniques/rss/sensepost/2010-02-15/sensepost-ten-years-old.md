After ten fascinating years, during which many people have contributed in so many ways to the place that is SensePost, by strange coincidence it falls on me to pen the words that mark our first decade in existence. To quote Robert Hunter: &#8220;What a long strange trip it&#8217;s been&#8221;. SensePost was officially founded on February 14, 2000. Of everyone who was involved at that time, I&#8217;m the only one still working here, which earns me the dubious honor of &#8216;oldest employee&#8217;. Do I get a gold watch? I meant to think much more over the last few weeks and months about how we should celebrate this day, or what I would write in a letter like this, but in the end (business being business) I&#8217;m writing this in a rush on a Sunday evening, with another three big things to complete before I allow myself to go to bed. Then again much of our success (in so far as we&#8217;ve been a success) happened in hurry on a Sunday night, so let&#8217;s not write this little piece off too soon, shall we?
