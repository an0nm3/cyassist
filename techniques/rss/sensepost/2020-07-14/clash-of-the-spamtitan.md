I recently tested an Internet facing Anti-Spam product called&nbsp; SpamTitan Gateway . As you could infer from the name of the product, this platform&#8217;s purpose was to detect Spam and or other malicious software sent via email. It has a lot of other features too as you could imagine from this type of product. In this post I will detail some vulnerabilities I discovered that ultimately lead to the ability to have unauthenticated remote code execution.
