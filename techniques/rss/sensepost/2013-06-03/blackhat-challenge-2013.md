One of the things we try and get across in our training &#8211; is that pen-testing requires  out of the box  thinking. It&#8217;s also about solving puzzles and making things work the way you want them to. It&#8217;s about identifying the small vulnerabilities (which are often easy to spot), and trying to leverage them into something useful. A key process we strive to do at SensePost, when performing these penetration tests, is about having fun.
