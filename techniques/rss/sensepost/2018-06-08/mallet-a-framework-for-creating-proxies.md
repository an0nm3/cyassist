Thanks to IoT and other developments, we&#8217;re having to review more and more non-HTTP protocols these days. While the hardware is interesting to look at, one can often find vulnerabilities at a network layer too. With many of the non-HTTP-only proxies having fallen out of maintenance or requiring a significant amount of custom code to be written for each unique protocol encountered, we decided to build a tool to make such work easier.
