In preparation for our  wireless training course at BlackHat Vegas in a few weeks , I spent some time updating the content on rogue/spoofed access points. What we mean by this are access points under your control, that you attempt to trick a user into connecting to, rather than the &#8220;unauthorised access points&#8221; Bob in Marketing bought and plugged into your internal network for his team to use. 
 I&#8217;ll discuss how to quickly get a rogue AP up on Kali that will allow you to start gathering some creds, specifically mail creds. Once you have that basic pattern down, setting up more complex attacks is fairly easy.
