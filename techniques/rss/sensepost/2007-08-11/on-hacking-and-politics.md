I meant to blog this whilst I was still in Vegas, but only got around 
to it now. Its arb, but worth a bit of thinking&#8230; Kenneth Geers&#8217; 
talk titled &#8216;Greetz from Room 101&#8217; was on which countries have the 
Top Ten most Orwellian computer networks. In his precis he asks 
&#8220;Could a cyber attack lead to a real-life government overthrow?&#8221; 
 I find these kinds of discussions really interesting, because of the 
significant role that information technology plays in today&#8217;s wars on 
crime and &#8216;terror&#8217;.  In such &#8220;wars&#8221; the lines between right and wrong 
are very loosely defined. As we saw clearly in South Africa today&#8217;s 
terrorist is tomorrow&#8217;s freedom fighter. Thus, a technology that 
could be used fight terror today, could just as easily be used to 
oppress freedom tomorrow. Technology will serve any master.
