Over the years we&#8217;ve offered almost all our tools, papers, presentations and other materials for free, albeit with a &#8220;registration required&#8221; proviso. The registration wall has been in place for some time now, and was used to track unique users as well as permit users to opt into SensePost mailruns. What we found though, is that registration is more of a hindrance than a benefit; it creates an artificial barrier with little reward. The data isn&#8217;t that useful to us and the added steps just an extra annoyance for users, and we wanted to streamline things a little.
