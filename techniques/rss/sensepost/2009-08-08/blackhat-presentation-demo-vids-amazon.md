 Goal 
 In the fourth installment of our BlackHat video series, we turned our attention to Amazon&#8217;s cloud platform and focused on their Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2) service specifically. 
 Theft of resources is the red-headed step-child of attack classes and doesn&#8217;t get much attention, but on cloud platforms where resources are shared amongst many users these attacks can have a very real impact. With this in mind, we wanted to show how EC2 was vulnerable to a number of resource theft attacks and the videos below demonstrate three separate attacks against EC2 that permit an attacker to boot up massive numbers of machines, steal computing time/bandwidth from other users and steal paid-for AMIs.
