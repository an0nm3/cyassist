I just got off a call with a client, and realised we need to think about how we report binary protections a bit more. More specifically the  ios info binary  command in  objection . They can be a pain to explain if not well understood, and even harder to remediate! Binary protections make exploitation attempts much harder so, naturally we want all of them on. However, as you&#8217;d see in this article, not everything can always be enabled and sometimes it&#8217;s hard to understand why.
