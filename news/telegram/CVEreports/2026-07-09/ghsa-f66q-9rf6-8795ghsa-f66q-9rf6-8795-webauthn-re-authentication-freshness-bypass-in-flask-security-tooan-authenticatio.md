GHSA-F66Q-9RF6-8795GHSA-f66q-9rf6-8795: WebAuthn Re-authentication Freshness Bypass in Flask-Security-TooAn authentication freshness bypass vulnerability exists in the WebAuthn re-authentication path of Flask-Security-Too versions 5.8.0 and 5.8.1. The flaw allows an authenticated attacker to elevate the freshness status of a victim session using their own WebAuthn credential, bypassing re-authentication constraints.
