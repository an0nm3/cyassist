Mukesh Bansal-led enterprise AI startup Nurix AI announced its plans to acquire Verloop.io to expand its offerings across both voice and chat-based AI solutions. The companies did not disclose the financial details of the transaction. With the acquisition, Nurix would look to bolster its flagship voice AI platform NuPlay by adding Verloop&#8217;s voice AI platform. As per a statement issued by Nurix, the acquired business currently automates more than 2 Cr customer interactions for its clients every month. Beside, the acquisition also expands Nurix’s ability to support enterprises that need AI agents across both synchronous voice interactions and asynchronous chat-led journeys. The acquisition comes at a time when competition in the enterprise AI market is shifting beyond standalone chatbots to full-stack AI platforms.
