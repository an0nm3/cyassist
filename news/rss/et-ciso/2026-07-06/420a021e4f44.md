India&#039;s SMEs are embracing digital tools, but cybersecurity preparedness lags. Cybercriminals are increasingly targeting smaller businesses, exploiting routine processes like invoice fraud. With over 12 lakh UPI fraud cases reported in FY25, the landscape has shifted, making cybersecurity a critical business resilience issue. Regulatory frameworks are now formalizing expectations, emphasizing practical steps over large budgets for enhanced cyber defense.
