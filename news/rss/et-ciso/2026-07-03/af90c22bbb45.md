Akamai and NVIDIA are joining forces to bolster AI infrastructure security. Their collaboration integrates Akamai&#039;s Guardicore Segmentation with NVIDIA&#039;s BlueField-4 STX, powered by DOCA. This aims to enforce workload-aware segmentation, monitor AI behavior, and contain threats at the infrastructure level without performance hits. The solution maps communications, applies identity-based policies, and enforces them directly in the data path, promising enhanced security for AI environments.
