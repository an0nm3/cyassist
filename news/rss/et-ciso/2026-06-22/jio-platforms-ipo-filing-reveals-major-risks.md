Jio Platforms, gearing up for a massive IPO, has revealed significant risks in its draft prospectus. These include challenges in securing future spectrum, potential network outages, cybersecurity threats, and the impact of evolving AI and data privacy regulations. The company also highlighted potential conflicts with other Reliance Group entities and the risk of measures limiting social media usage affecting data consumption.
