A massive, coordinated network of 152 malicious Google Chrome browser extensions has been dismantled after researchers caught the operation generating fake organic Google search traffic. Socket’s Threat Research Team discovered the operation spanning 38 separate Chrome Web Store publisher accounts and tracing back to three primary brand backends: tabplugins[.]com, yowgames[.]com, and chromewallpaper[.]com.
