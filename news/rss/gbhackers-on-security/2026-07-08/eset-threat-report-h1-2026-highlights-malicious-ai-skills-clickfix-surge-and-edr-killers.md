ESET’s H1 2026 threat report shows attackers accelerating the use of familiar playbooks with AI-flavored lures, social engineering, and defense evasion rather than inventing entirely new ones. The clearest signals are the rise of malicious AI skills, a doubled ClickFix footprint, first-wave AI-powered Android malware, record QR-code phishing, and a growing ecosystem of EDR killers.
