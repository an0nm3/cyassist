Hindi actress Preity Zinta has secured an ad interim injunction from the Bombay HC against Google, X, Meta, and several AI platforms over deepfakes, chatbot personas, and merchandise that misuse her name, image, and voice without consent. Justice Madhav J. Jamdar passed the order, directing Google, X, and Meta to take down or block access to roughly 275 infringing URLs within 72 hours. Zinta first approached the HC in June seeking permission to sue intermediaries and other parties. Justice Abhay Ahuja granted that permission.
