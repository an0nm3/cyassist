WhatsApp banned 72,14,702 Indian accounts between May 1 and May 31, 2026, according to its latest monthly transparency report released under the Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021. Of these, 13,79,438 accounts were proactively blocked before any user complaints were received. User complaints and action taken: The report shows that WhatsApp received 19,859 user grievances in India during the month but took action on only 399 accounts. Most complaints were ban appeals (10,590), followed by other support requests (8,378), account support (519), safety complaints (210) and product support (162). WhatsApp restored or banned accounts in response to complaints, including 151 cases involving ban appeals, 244 following other support requests, 3 in account support cases and 1 in product support.
