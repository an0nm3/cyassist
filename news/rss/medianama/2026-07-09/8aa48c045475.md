Airtel added 2.93 million wireless (mobile) subscribers during the month, followed by Jio with 2.15 million. Vodafone Idea added 1.21 lakh subscribers, while BSNL lost 1.05 lakh users. MTNL and Reliance Communications together lost fewer than 4,000 subscribers. Overall subscriber growth continues: India&#8217;s wireless subscriber base, which includes mobile and fixed wireless access (FWA) users, increased by 5.5 million to 1.294 billion in May from 1.289 billion in April, a monthly growth of 0.43%.
