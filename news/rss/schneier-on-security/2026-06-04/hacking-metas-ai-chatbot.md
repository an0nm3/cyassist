Hackers are  convincing  Meta&#8217;s AI support chatbot to let them take over other peoples&#8217; accounts: 
  A  video  posted on X showed the step-by-step process to hack someone&#8217;s Instagram account. The hacker allegedly used a VPN to spoof the targets&#8217; presumed location to avoid triggering Instagram&#8217;s automated account protections. Then, the hacker opened a chat with Meta AI Support Assistant and asked the bot to add a new email address to the target&#8217;s account. The chatbot can be seen sending a verification code to the email address provided by the hacker; the hacker then shares the verification code with the chatbot, which prompts the chatbot to show a button to &#8220;Reset Password.&#8221; The hacker enters a new password and takes over the victim&#8217;s account...
