Last week, national security agencies from the Five Eyes&#8212;that&#8217;s the rich, English-language-speaking countries club&#8212;jointly released a  statement  warning of the increasing cyber risks of AI models: in particular, their ability to autonomously hack into systems and networks. The statement was more measured than some of the  breathless headlines  about it, and the advice they gave is pretty much the standard advice everyone gives&#8212;albeit with newfound urgency. 
 Internet risks are nothing new, and cyberattacks&#8212;both large and small&#8212;have been a significant issue since long before the current crop of generative AI models...
